About Imad Jomaa

Imad Jomaa is a visionary entrepreneur, investor, and digital transformation leader recognized for reshaping the media and technology industries in the Middle East and North Africa. As the Founder and Chairman of JGroup, he has built a diversified holding company operating across advertising, media, technology, and real estate.

Early Life & Personal Background

Imad Zakaria Jomaa was born on November 19, 1978 in Sidon (Saida), Lebanon. A proud Lebanese national, he pursued his higher education at the American University of Beirut, where he earned dual bachelor's degrees in Economics and Chemistry and went on to complete a Master's in Financial Economics.

During his academic years, he distinguished himself and eventually founded JGroup in 2003, shortly after completing his master's studies. Jomaa is based in Beirut & Dubai, balancing his professional commitments with a deep dedication to family. He is a father of four children, as publicly shared in his social media posts.

A Journey of Vision and Growth

Imad's entrepreneurial path began in the early 2000s with the launch of Promofix, a digital media house that quickly became a trusted advertising partner in Lebanon and across the GCC. Promofix was one of the first agencies to deliver regional programmatic solutions and paved the way for JGroup's expansion into new markets.

Under his leadership, JGroup grew into a powerhouse with ventures spanning multiple sectors. His strategic foresight has led to landmark partnerships with global tech giants such as Google, TikTok, Amazon, and Snap, ensuring regional access to advanced programmatic and digital advertising solutions.

Leading Innovation Through Strategic Partnerships

With a deep understanding of regional dynamics and a bold vision for the future, Imad Jomaa has positioned JGroup as a catalyst for change. His focus on technological integration, performance-driven media, and market expansion continues to redefine how businesses in MENA connect with consumers.
